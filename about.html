About us

Our Mission

Crypto Borrow DApp is on a mission to provide accessible and collateral-free cryptocurrency lending opportunities to users on the Binance Smart Chain. We believe in financial inclusion and aim to empower users with the ability to borrow crypto assets without the need for traditional collateral.

How It Works

Our DApp utilizes smart contracts on the Binance Smart Chain to enable borrowers to request cryptocurrency loans. Loans are approved based on user creditworthiness and history. Users can select their loan amount, interest rate, and terms. Once approved, the borrowed funds are disbursed directly to the user's BSC wallet. Users are required to make regular repayments, including both principal and interest, according to the agreed-upon schedule.
