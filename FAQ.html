FAQ


  1. On which basis Borrowed amount is approved ?

    2. Based upon your Account History, How old your account is it, How often you used it, We assign a CFL credit Score that is related your account. That determies your Loan Approval Chances.



  2. How I can check my account's CFL credit score ?

    3. For that you can visit Check Score Page. And if your credit score meets our criteria means you are eligible for Collateral Free Loan.



  3. How and When my credit score is updated ?

    4. Your CFL credit score is determined based upon your account's activity. And you can check your CFL once in 2 days. If your CFL credit score is less than the mentioned criteria, Then we prefer you to use your account more often and build some account history. After that check it again.



  4. Is gas fees used to proceed transaction is refundable ?

    5. Short Answer No. Long Answer Yes.

    As you know the gas fees is charged by the Blockchain to proceed any transaction / event over the network, And that is non refundable. But If you used our and spent more than 0.01 BNB, then we arranged a Airdrop equivalet to 8 USD for your wallet with which you made the transaction. Claim Airdrop Compensation. But if your compensation amount is beyond 8 USD, then you can raise your request.



  5. Is there is gas fees for Airdrop Compensation ?

    6. Yes, As I told earlier for every transaction Blockchain charges gas fees. So to keep it in notice we are giving 8 USD equivalet tokens Compensation which also includes Airdrop Claim Gas fees compensaion too.
